About us

Alembic is an Australian-based technology consultancy that is passionate about delivering software that promotes speed to market and simplicity in the best languages possible. We are focussed on delivering Elixir and GraphQL projects.

What we do

We want to work with small to mid-sized startups as well as larger enterprise customers who are making bold and brave ‘newer’ technology choices like Elixir and GraphQL. These companies have members who aware of the power of functional programming languages and understand Ruby and Rails provides only so much return, and that the power of Elixir supports hyper-flexible businesses with very very fast time to production for their ever changing requirements and growing customer bases.

We want our market to understand we are part of the startup community as well, and that we want to grow with them.

We want to set the tone that Alembic will always be on the front foot when it comes to implementing the best technology has to offer.

Our philosophy

Hey, really, we love Ruby. We've been delivering Ruby projects since the language first came onto the tech scene. However, we believe Ruby only takes you so far. From our experience we've seen that once a codebase gets big the efficiencies of using Ruby begin to diminish. Things start to slow down and codebases become increasingly difficult to maintain - especially with large-scale, distributed, highly available, high traffic systmes and applications.

Functional programming is a great way to combat these issues. We can absolutely show you how you can begin to incorporate Elixir and GraphQL into your technology stack where is makes the most sense.

Process

We have been building software in many different languages and industries since the 80's, and we are seriously passionate about doing the simplest thing possible to get software into production.

We have an active open source project that is an implementation of GraphQL written in Elixir http://graphql-elixir.org/

If you want to build your own Elixir development team we can work alongside you, pairing with people who are learning. We prefer to enable technologists through delivering real projects, but we can also provide time-boxed proofs of concept that will help you determine how much Elixir you can handle.

We can work directly with you and your teams to deliver projects, or we can deliver entire projects for you.
